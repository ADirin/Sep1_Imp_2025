# Course Evaluation Scheme (Final Grade: 0–5)

**Grading scale:**  
- **0.0 = Fail**  
- **5.0 = Maximum score**

The course project is completed in **teams** and divided into **four sprints**, each lasting **two weeks**.  
Teams must submit a **Sprint Review Report** at the end of each sprint.  
At the end of the course, students must submit a **Self & Peer Review** evaluating individual contributions.

---

## Overview of Weighting

| Component | Max Internal Points | Weight | Contribution to Final Grade |
|----------|---------------------|--------|-----------------------------|
| Sprint Tasks (4 sprints) | 40 (10 × 4) | 60% | 3.0 |
| Final Project Outcome (Team) | 10 | 20% | 1.0 |
| Self & Peer Review (Individual) | 10 | 20% | 1.0 |
| **Total** | — | **100%** | **5.0** |

---

## 1. Sprint Tasks (4 Sprints) — **0–3.0 points**

- The project consists of **4 sprints**
- Each sprint task is graded on a **0–10 point scale**
- Each sprint contributes **0–0.75 points** to the final course grade
