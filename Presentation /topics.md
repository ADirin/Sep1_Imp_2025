## Presentation Topics: AI Impact on Software Engineering, DevOps, and Scrum

The following topics are intended for a **25-minute student presentation** in the Software Engineering Process (SEP) course.  
Each topic focuses on the **impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI)** on **software development lifecycle, Agile/Scrum practices, and DevOps**.

---

### 1. AI-Enhanced Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC)
**Focus:**  
How AI tools transform each phase of the SDLC, from requirements engineering to maintenance.

**Key aspects:**
- AI in requirements analysis and documentation
- AI-assisted design and code generation
- AI in testing, deployment, and maintenance
- Benefits and risks of AI-driven SDLC

---

### 2. The Role of AI in Agile and Scrum Teams
**Focus:**  
How AI supports or challenges Agile values and Scrum practices.

**Key aspects:**
- AI-assisted sprint planning and backlog refinement
- Effort estimation and velocity prediction
- AI as a virtual Scrum assistant
- Impact on team autonomy and decision-making

---

### 3. AI-Driven DevOps: From CI/CD to AIOps
**Focus:**  
The evolution of DevOps pipelines through AI and automation.

**Key aspects:**
- AI in CI/CD pipelines
- Predictive monitoring and incident detection
- AIOps vs traditional DevOps
- Human-in-the-loop DevOps models

---

### 4. Code Generation with AI: Productivity Booster or Technical Debt?
**Focus:**  
Evaluating AI-based code generation tools and their impact on software quality.

**Key aspects:**
- Developer productivity gains
- Security, correctness, and licensing risks
- Maintainability and technical debt
- Long-term impact on software projects

---

### 5. AI in Software Testing and Quality Assurance
**Focus:**  
The use of AI to enhance testing processes and software quality.

**Key aspects:**
- AI-generated test cases
- Intelligent regression testing
- Defect prediction
- Limitations of AI-based testing

---

### 6. AI-Assisted Requirements Engineering
**Focus:**  
How AI improves requirement elicitation, analysis, and validation.

**Key aspects:**
- Natural language processing for requirements
- Detecting ambiguity and inconsistency
- Stakeholder communication support
- Ethical risks and bias in AI-assisted requirements

---

### 7. Ethical and Responsible AI in Software Engineering
**Focus:**  
Ethical challenges and responsibilities when using AI in software development.

**Key aspects:**
- Bias, fairness, and transparency
- Data privacy and security
- Accountability in AI-assisted development
- Responsible AI practices in Agile teams

---

### 8. AI and Project Management in Software Engineering
**Focus:**  
The impact of AI on planning, monitoring, and risk management.

**Key aspects:**
- AI-based effort estimation
- Risk prediction and mitigation
- Project health monitoring dashboards
- Limitations of AI in complex team environments

---

### 9. Impact of AI on Software Engineering Roles and Skills
**Focus:**  
How AI is reshaping software engineering roles and required competencies.

- New skill requirements and AI literacy

---

### 10. AI-Powered Continuous Improvement in Agile Teams
**Focus:**  
Using AI to support retrospectives and continuous improvement.

**Key aspects:**
- AI analysis of sprint and workflow data
- Identifying bottlenecks and inefficiencies
- Data-driven retrospectives
- Risks of excessive automation
